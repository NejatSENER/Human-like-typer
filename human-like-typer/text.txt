# MarketNest — General Marketplace Platform

## What the App Does & Who It's For

MarketNest is a multi-vendor marketplace where independent sellers list products across any category and buyers discover, compare, and purchase them. Think Amazon meets Etsy — broad category coverage, seller storefronts, buyer reviews, and a robust checkout flow. The platform earns revenue through a per-sale commission (configurable per category) and optional promoted listing fees.

Three parties interact simultaneously: buyers who browse and purchase; sellers who manage storefronts, inventory, and orders; and admins who moderate listings, manage disputes, configure commission rates, and view platform analytics.

## User Roles

Guest:
- Browse all published listings, search, filter, sort
- View product detail pages and seller profiles
- Cannot add to cart, wishlist, or checkout
- Sees a "Sign in to purchase" prompt on cart/checkout attempt

Buyer (authenticated):
- All guest capabilities
- Add to cart and wishlist
- Complete checkout (guest checkout not supported — account required for order history)
- Track orders, request returns, leave reviews
- Manage saved addresses and payment methods
- Cannot list products

Seller (authenticated, separate role from buyer — but one account can hold both):
- All buyer capabilities
- Access to seller dashboard: create/edit/delete listings, manage orders, view analytics
- Upload product images, set prices, manage inventory
- Configure store profile (banner, bio, policies)
- Respond to buyer reviews and messages
- Withdraw earnings (Stripe Connect payouts)
- Cannot access other sellers' dashboards or admin tools

Admin:
- Full platform access
- Approve, reject, or remove any listing
- Suspend or ban any user account
- Configure global settings: commission rates per category, featured slots, homepage curation
- Manage disputes between buyers and sellers
- View platform analytics: GMV, orders, top sellers, top categories, refund rate

## 3. Authentication & Sessions

**Methods:** Email + password for all roles. Google OAuth for buyers. No social login for sellers (to enforce business account verification). Apple Sign In available for buyers on mobile web.

**Buyer signup flow:**
1. User clicks "Sign Up" → modal slides up (translateY(20px)→0, 250ms ease-out)
2. Fields: full name, email, password (min 8 chars, 1 number, 1 uppercase), newsletter opt-in
3. POST `/api/auth/register` — creates account with role `buyer`, sends verification email
4. Unverified buyers can browse and add to cart but not checkout. Banner: "Verify your email to place orders."
5. Verification link expires 48 hours. Resend option available.

**Seller onboarding (separate flow from buyer signup):**
1. Existing buyer clicks "Start Selling" in account menu → navigated to `/seller/apply`
2. Step 1 — Store details: store name (unique, validated in real-time), store description, category focus (multi-select), country
3. Step 2 — Identity verification: legal name, date of birth, business type (Individual / LLC / Corporation), tax ID (optional, required for payouts above threshold)
4. Step 3 — Payout setup: Stripe Connect onboarding (embedded Stripe flow)
5. On completion: seller role added to existing account, seller dashboard unlocked. Email confirmation sent.
6. Admin review not required — auto-approved after Stripe Connect verification.

**Sessions:** JWT in httpOnly cookie, 14-day expiry for buyers/sellers (with silent refresh token), 8-hour for admins. Rate limiting: 10 failed login attempts → 20-minute lockout.

**Password reset:** Forgot-password email flow, token expires 1 hour, no email enumeration (same UI response whether account exists or not).

## Search, Filter & Sort

**Full-text search:** On `Product.title`, `Product.description`, `SellerProfile.store_name`. PostgreSQL `tsvector` with GIN index. Results ranked by: text relevance × recency boost × sales velocity boost.

**Filters:** Category (tree, multi-select), price range (min/max), condition, avg_rating (minimum), free shipping (boolean), on sale (boolean), seller country, in-stock only.

**Sort options:** Relevance (default on search), Newest, Price Low→High, Price High→Low, Best Rated (avg_rating desc), Most Sold (sales_count desc).

**Category pages:** Default sort: Most Sold. All filters available.

## Real-Time Features

**Cart sync:** Cart state stored server-side. If buyer is logged in on two devices, both see the same cart. Updates via PATCH debounced 500ms.

**Stock reservation:** When a buyer places an order, stock is decremented immediately. If payment fails, stock is restored within 15 minutes via a background job.

**Order status push:** Sellers marking an order as "Shipped" triggers an immediate email to the buyer. No WebSocket needed — email + buyer polling on the orders page every 60 seconds updates the status badge.

**Flash sale countdown:** Timer on the homepage is client-side, counting down to the sale end timestamp returned from the API. No SSE required.

**Price drop alerts:** If a wishlisted product's price drops, a background job (run nightly) sends email notifications to all users who have wishlisted it.

**Empty states:**
- Cart empty: illustration + "Your cart is empty." + "Start Shopping" CTA + recommended products row
- No search results: "No results for '[query]'." + spelling suggestions + "Browse categories" CTA
- No orders yet: "You haven't placed any orders yet." + "Start Shopping" CTA
- Seller no listings: "You haven't listed any products yet." + "Create your first listing" CTA
- Wishlist empty: "Nothing saved yet. Click ♡ on any product you love."

**Error handling:**
- Out of stock on checkout: inline error on cart item — "Sorry, [Product] is out of stock. Please remove it to continue." Remove button highlighted.
- Payment failure: Stripe error mapped to user-friendly message below card form. Order not created. Cart preserved.
- Product removed by admin: in cart, item shows "This item is no longer available." with remove link.
- 401 on protected route: redirect to `/login?redirect=[path]`
- 404: custom branded page with search bar + featured products below
- 500: "Something went wrong. Please try again." with retry button. Cart state preserved in localStorage as fallback.

**Toast notifications (top-right, 3s auto-dismiss):**
- Green: "Added to cart", "Item saved to wishlist", "Order placed!", "Review submitted"
- Amber: "Promo code applied — saving $N"
- Red: "Out of stock", "Payment failed", "Couldn't save item"

## 10. Payments

**Stripe integration:**
- Buyers: Stripe PaymentIntent created server-side at checkout, card charged via Stripe Elements on the frontend
- Sellers: Stripe Connect (Express accounts). Platform collects full payment from buyer, then transfers `seller_net` to seller's Stripe account after a 7-day holding period (fraud protection)
- Commission: deducted automatically at transfer time. Configurable per category in admin
- Refunds: admin or seller can trigger full/partial refund via Stripe API. Refunded amount deducted from seller's pending balance
- Payouts: seller requests payout → Stripe Connect transfer to linked bank account. Minimum payout $10. Processing time 2–3 business days
- Webhooks: `payment_intent.succeeded`, `payment_intent.payment_failed`, `transfer.paid`, `account.updated` (Stripe Connect status changes)
